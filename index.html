Crime has been declining

Since 2018, San Francisco has seen a 14.7% decrease in total reported crimes. The trend shows a significant drop during 2020, likely influenced by pandemic-related factors.

Crime Trends Over Time

Shifting Patterns

While overall crime has decreased, the patterns reveal interesting shifts. Violent crime has decreased by 25.1%, while property crime fell by 18.3%.

The story continues

While the data shows encouraging trends in crime reduction, the work is far from over. Communities, law enforcement, and policymakers must continue collaborating to ensure San Francisco remains safe for all residents.
